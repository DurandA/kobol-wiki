### LED indicators

LED Name|Color|Description
---|---|---
LED1|green|SYS power
LED2|green|Peripheral power
LED3|green|HDD power
LED4|blue|System ON
LED5|bule|HDD activity
LED6|green|System Status
LED7|red|System Error
LED8|orange|Battery Charge
